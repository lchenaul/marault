Data Visibility Audit

Fix messy data and give owners real visibility. We define what matters, clean and consolidate your data, and deliver one executive dashboard with a clear insight summary.

Establish the source of truth

How confident are you in the numbers you’re using to make decisions? When data is messy or inconsistent, teams slow down and leadership hesitates.

This audit defines what matters, cleans and consolidates your data, and establishes a reliable reporting foundation. We identify where reporting breaks down—duplicate tables, inconsistent joins, unclear metric definitions—and resolve the issues that prevent teams from trusting the numbers. The result is a single executive dashboard and a concise insight summary: what leadership should watch, what needs attention, and where performance is shifting.
